Eobak's Workout Plan

The workout plan that will GUARANTEE you success in the gym!.

The Plan:

Monday - Push Day

  • Incline Dumbell Press OR Incline Smith Machine

    • 3 sets of 6-10 reps

  • Chest Press OR Chest Press (Cables)

    • 3 sets of 8-15 reps

  • Cable Y raise

    • 2 sets of 10-15 reps

  • Shoulder Press

    • 2 sets of 6-12 reps

  • Cross Cable Tricep Extension

    • 3 sets of 8-12 reps

  • Overhead Tricep Extension

    • 3 sets of 8-12 reps

Tuesday - Pull Day

  • Pull Ups OR Lat Pulldown

    • 3 sets of 8-12 reps

  • Seated Rows

    • 3 sets of 6-10 reps

  • Face Pulls OR Rear Delt Machine

    • 2 sets of 8-12 reps

  • Incline Bicep Curls

    • 2 sets of 8-12 reps

  • Seated Ez Bar Bicep Curls

    • 2 sets of 10-15 reps

  • Lat Pullover

    • 3 sets of 8-12 reps

  • Shrugs

    • 3 sets of 8-12 reps
